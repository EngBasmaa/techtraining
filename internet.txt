>> The Classes Summarization of 'internet':

The Internet is a worldwide network of computers that communicate with each other using standardized protocols. It is not controlled by a single person or organization. 

Data is transmitted on the Internet through various mediums, such as Ethernet cables, fiber optic cables, and wireless signals. 

IP addresses and DNS are used to identify and locate computers on the Internet. 

Information is transferred in the form of packets, which can take different routes during transmission. 

HTTP is the standard protocol for transferring webpages over the Internet. 

Cryptography and SSL/TLS ensure secure communication on the Internet. 

Cybersecurity involves protecting against criminal activity on the Internet.
